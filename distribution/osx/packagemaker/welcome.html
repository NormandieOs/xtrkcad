XTrkCAD is a CAD program for designing model railroad layouts.

Using it, you can:

  • Design layouts in any scale and gauge.
  • Use predefined libraries of many popular brands of track and structures.
  • Create your own custom components.
  • Modify, extend and join tracks and turnouts much like working with flex-track.
  • Test your design by running trains.
  • Print your design in a scale of your choice, including 1:1 scale.

Learning XTrkCAD is easy with the extensive on-line help and demonstrations.
